Training & Nutrition Tips

Here are a few key habits that fuel strong performance, these tips are simple but consistent.

Hydrate

Drink at least half your body weight in oz of water a day (more during training!).

Fuel Smart

Focus on lean protein, complex carbs, and recovery snacks.

Rest

Sleep is your secret weapon. 8 hours minimum for muscle repair.
